About Josh Fernandez

Hello! I am Josh Fernandez - a fourth-year Software Systems student at Simon Fraser University, also pursuing a minor in Interactive Arts and Technology. I want to pursue a career in software engineering and front-end web development.

These are the core set of skills and abilities that I've acquired through my university experiences:

  • C++, Java, and Object-Oriented Programming
  • Git, Version Control, and Workflow
  • Networking and Server-Client Interaction
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Front-End Website Development
  • Team Collaboration and Agile Development

I am an open-minded, assertive, and hardworking programmer, hoping to pursue a job that will not only challenge my technical and computer programming skills but will also test my communication and leadership abilities. I have been a secretary and director of marketing for the Software Systems Student Society, a Welcome Leader for incoming SFU first-year students, and a volunteer through SFU's Student Ambassador Program.

I am passionate about learning new things, honing my acquired skills and talents, and experiencing new job environments. If you want to get in contact with me, please don't hesitate to fill out the form below.
